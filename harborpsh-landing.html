The Problem

PSH providers are drowning in
operational complexity

California's permanent supportive housing operators manage vulnerable populations with life-critical services — but they're doing it with tools built for a different era.

📋

Fragmented Systems

Tenant data lives across Excel, paper files, email chains, and disconnected portals. Case workers waste hours searching for information that should be at their fingertips.

⚠️

Compliance Risk

HUD reporting, HMIS data entry, annual recertifications, and HQS inspections — one missed deadline can jeopardize funding for an entire program.

🔒

HIPAA Exposure

Sensitive health and behavioral data is shared via unencrypted email and stored in unsecured spreadsheets. One breach could mean six-figure fines and lost trust.

The Platform

One system for everything
your PSH program needs

HarborPSH is built from the ground up for the unique workflows of permanent supportive housing — not adapted from generic property management software.

01

🏠 Tenant Management

Complete tenant lifecycle tracking from intake through transition. Demographics, unit assignments, lease compliance, and recertification schedules in a unified view.

02

📝 Case Coordination

HIPAA-compliant case notes, service plans, and care team coordination. AI-powered summaries help case workers stay on top of complex caseloads without losing context.

03

📊 Compliance Engine

Automated tracking for HUD reporting, HMIS data requirements, HQS inspections, and annual recertifications. Get alerts before deadlines — not after you've missed them.

04

🤖 AI Assistant

Ask questions about your portfolio in natural language. "Which tenants have recertifications due this month?" "Show me units with open maintenance requests." Instant answers, no report building.
